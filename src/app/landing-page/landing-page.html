How events get on the calendar

Club execs and team captains submit events in under a minute. We review regularly to keep things accurate.

  1. 1

    Open the form

    Use the official submission portal to send in your event.

  2. 2

    Add the details

    Name, date/time, location (e.g., Room 225), and a short description.

  3. 3

    We publish it

    Submissions are reviewed to prevent duplicates and keep information consistent.
